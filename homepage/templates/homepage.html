With the growing use of Machine Learning, it is more important than ever for everyone to have a base understanding of how the technology works. Finance Bro serves as an example of how we can present Machine Learning in a way that makes it understandable not just to those who have studied machine learning.

What is a Neural Network? Why do we use them?

The human brain does a really good job of thinking, specifically drawing patterns and making generalizations. Neural Networks attempt to recreate this process through an algorithm that mimics the activity of our brain

"Much of current machine learning research has lost its connection to problems of import to the larger world of science and society"

-Kiri Wagstaff, Machine Learning that matters.

Where other research fails, Finance Bro succeeds. Though the goal is education, the statistical findings come from real data that is relevant in our larger world
